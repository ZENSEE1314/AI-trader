Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe? Can the bot withdraw my funds?

No. Your API keys are configured with trade-only permissions. The bot cannot withdraw, transfer, or access your funds in any way. Your capital stays on your exchange at all times. API keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM.

Which exchanges are supported?

Currently we support Bitunix Futures. You can connect up to 3 API keys simultaneously, each with independent risk settings (leverage, position size, max loss).

How does the AI decide when to trade?

Our engine scans 40+ coins every 10 minutes using 7 indicators in confluence: RSI, EMA crossovers, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Stochastic, Volume analysis, and Smart Money Concepts (SMC). A trade only fires when multiple indicators align on the same direction.

What is the difference between the Signal Channel and the Auto Bot?

The Signal Channel delivers real-time trade alerts to a private Telegram group. You execute manually. The Auto Bot does everything automatically: it receives the signal and places the trade on your exchange with your risk settings, including entry, stop loss, and 3 take profit levels.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes. There are no contracts or lock-in periods. You can cancel from your dashboard at any time. Your access remains active until the end of your billing period.
