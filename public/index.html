Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe? Can the bot withdraw my funds?

No. Your API keys are configured with trade-only permissions. The bot cannot withdraw, transfer, or access your funds in any way. Your capital stays on your exchange at all times. API keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM.

Which exchanges are supported?

We support Binance USDT-M Futures and Bitunix Futures. You can connect up to 3 API keys simultaneously, each with independent risk settings (leverage, position size, max loss).

How does the AI decide when to trade?

Our engine scans 40+ coins every 3 minutes using Smart Money Concepts (SMC) with multi-timeframe confluence (daily, 4H, 15M, 3M, 1M). A trade only fires when structure, bias, and volume all align. Trailing stop-loss automatically locks in profits as the trade moves in your favour.

How does the profit split work?

There are no monthly fees. When a trade closes in profit, 60% goes to you and 40% is the platform fee. Losing trades cost you nothing extra. Your 60% share is automatically added to your cash wallet.

Can I stop trading at any time?

Yes. Simply disable your API key from the dashboard. There are no contracts or lock-in periods. Open positions will continue to be managed until they close.
